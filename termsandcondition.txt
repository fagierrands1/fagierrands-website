SCOPE OF SERVICES
Fagi Errands is a digital platform designed to provide users with on-demand errand services through a mobile app available on Android and iOS. The services offered include, but are not limited to, shopping errands, banking tasks, parcel and cargo deliveries, and home services such as plumbing, electrical work, and landscaping. These services are carried out by trained, vetted personnel who are coordinated in real-time via the application.
PAYMENTS AND FEES
All payments must be made through the app’s secure, integrated platforms, which include M-Pesa, Visa, and Global Pay. For shopping errands, clients are required to pay 30% of the estimated shopping amount upfront as a commitment fee. The remaining 70%, along with a delivery fee, becomes payable upon successful delivery. Receipts from the shopping venue are scanned and uploaded to the app by the service provider, and clients receive an auto-generated digital receipt via the app. For services like plumbing, electrical work, and landscaping, clients are required to pay a flat facilitation fee of KES 500 upfront. This fee is redeemable upon successful service completion. All delivery errands are priced dynamically based on distance and real-time traffic, with cost estimates provided before confirmation.
CANCELLATIONS AND REFUNDS
In cases where a client cancels a service after it has been assigned, a full refund may not be guaranteed. For non-perishable goods, items will be stored at a designated location free of charge for up to three days. From the fourth to the seventh day, a daily storage fee will apply. Items unclaimed after seven days will be considered forfeited, and a 30% processing fee will be deducted from the original payment. Perishable goods must be collected within their natural freshness window, and any losses due to spoilage are the responsibility of the client. No refunds will be issued for perishables that are not collected on time.
LIABILITY AND DAMAGE POLICY
Clients are required to declare the condition, value, and nature of goods being transported, particularly for package or cargo deliveries. For security, item value will not be disclosed to service providers. Clients are encouraged to upload clear photographs of their goods before handover. If an item is damaged, the client must report it immediately through the app’s “Report Issue” feature before completing the final payment. Reports must include a description of the damage, supporting images or videos, and the timestamp of the incident. Fagi Errands will conduct a full investigation within seven days, which may include reviewing service logs, contacting both parties, and analyzing uploaded media. If the fault lies with the service provider, the company will facilitate compensation. If the client failed to provide accurate information or if the damage was due to external, uncontrollable factors such as riots or traffic accidents, no compensation will be issued.
